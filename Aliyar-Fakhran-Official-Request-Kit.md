# Aliyar Fakhran — Official Archive & Honors Request Kit

This file provides reusable request templates for contacting the **owning government or archive** for official records, award citations, photographs, medal registers, appointment decrees, and related collateral for a historical diplomat/public servant.

**Primary subject:** `میرزا آقاخان ممتحن‌الملک علی‌یار فخران` / `Mirza Agha Khan Mumtahen-ul-Mulk Aliyar Fakhran`

---

## 1) Target honors and record owners

| Honor / Record | Likely owning government or archive | What to request |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Orden de Isabel la Católica** (Spain) | Government of Spain / Ministry of Foreign Affairs / honors or archive office | Award citation, class of award, date of conferment, decree, register entry, scan/photo |
| **Order of St. Stanislaus** (Russian Empire) | Russian historical state archives / imperial foreign affairs records | Imperial register entry, class, date, diplomatic correspondence, medal documentation |
| **Order of the Medjidie** (Ottoman Empire) | Türkiye State Archives / Ottoman archive collections | Decree, register entry, medal class, record of conferment |
| **Order of the Lion and Sun** (Iran) | Iran MFA archives / National Archives of Iran | Royal decree, award record, class, appointment file, image or scan |
| **Consular / embassy service records** | Iran MFA archives, relevant diplomatic archives in Russia/Georgia/Türkiye | Service postings, diplomatic dispatches, appointment letters, consular records |

---

## 2) Materials to request

When writing to any archive or ministry, ask for the following if available:

- Official **award citation** or **decree of conferment**
- **Register entry** showing name, title, class/rank, and date
- **Portraits**, official photographs, or file images
- **Scans of certificates**, warrants, medals, ribbons, or insignia records
- **Embassy / consulate / foreign ministry records** tied to the individual
- **Usage and reproduction permissions** for public historical publication
- Required **credit line** and citation format for reuse

## 3) Reusable English request template

> **Subject:** Request for archival records and publication permission concerning historical diplomat Mirza Agha Khan Mumtahen-ul-Mulk Aliyar Fakhran

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to respectfully request access to, or information about, any official records held by your institution concerning **Mirza Agha Khan Mumtahen-ul-Mulk Aliyar Fakhran** (also written as **Aliyar Fakhran**), an Iranian diplomat and public official active in the late Qajar and early Pahlavi periods.

I am preparing a **historical and educational documentation project** concerning his public service, diplomatic career, and official honors. The materials are intended for careful use in **public-history writing**, including a family-supported historical profile, educational blog and newsletter material, book development, and related public-facing documentation.

If available, I would be grateful for copies, references, or catalog information regarding:

1. official award or decoration records;
2. the class and date of conferment;
3. citation texts, warrants, decrees, or register entries;
4. official portraits or photographic materials;
5. diplomatic, embassy, consular, or foreign ministry records associated with his service; and
6. any guidance regarding reproduction permission, attribution, and licensing for educational publication.

Known associated forms of his name include:

- **Mirza Agha Khan Mumtahen-ul-Mulk**
- **Aliyar Fakhran**
- **میرزا آقاخان ممتحن‌الملک علی‌یار فخران**

Known associated distinctions and service references include:

- service in **St. Petersburg / Leningrad**, **Tbilisi**, and **Batumi**;
- Iranian diplomatic and consular posts in the Russian and Caucasus regions; and
- awards connected to Spain, Iran, the Ottoman Empire, and Tsarist Russia.

If needed, I can provide proof of family connection, project background, and identification.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I would be grateful for any assistance, catalog references, or referral to the appropriate archival department.

Sincerely,  
**[Your Full Name]**  
**[City, Country]**  
**[Email]**  
**[Phone, optional]**
