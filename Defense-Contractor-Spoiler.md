## The Financial Asymmetry
-   **The W2 Reality:** A contractor employee makes **k**.
-   **The Contractor Profit:** The firm bills/profits **M** on that head.
-   **The US Gov Cost:** The taxpayer pays **M** for the project capability.

## The Efficiency Gap
-   **The Waste:** Where does the other M go? Bureaucracy, overhead, management, and planning (50-year horizons).
-   **The Result:**
    -   We pay for a Ferrari/F-35.
    -   We get a Corolla/Camry performance.
    -   The enemy (ISIS of the Future) fights with Toyota trucks and wins on ROI.

## The Correction
-   **Direct Command:** Cut the middlemen (the Contractor Firm). 
-   **Value:** Pay the talent (the k guy) directly or execute via smaller, leaner Kill Chain cells.
-   **Truth:** This financial bloat is why we have the "Execution Gap".
