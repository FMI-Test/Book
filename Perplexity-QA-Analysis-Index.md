## Recommended Workflow
1. Open one source link from [Perplexity-Research-Links.md](Perplexity-Research-Links.md).
2. Copy full Q&A into the matching entry in [Perplexity-QA-Full-Archive.md](Perplexity-QA-Full-Archive.md).
3. Mark capture status for that entry.
4. Record key evidence, assumptions, and confidence.
5. Update thematic findings in [Perplexity-QA-Insights-Report.md](Perplexity-QA-Insights-Report.md).
6. Repeat until all entries are complete.

## Completion Criteria
- Every entry in [Perplexity-QA-Full-Archive.md](Perplexity-QA-Full-Archive.md) has full question and full answer text captured.
- Every entry has a confidence level and evidence notes.
- [Perplexity-QA-Insights-Report.md](Perplexity-QA-Insights-Report.md) includes final patterns, contradictions, and action items.
